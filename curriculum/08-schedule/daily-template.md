## Why this template works

- Forces you to **commit numbers** (questions, hours) so motivation isn't mood-dependent.
- "Mistake pattern" and "Hardest insight" tags are *the* compounding fields — re-read them weekly.
- The "Tomorrow's first task" line saves 15 min of decision fatigue tomorrow morning.
- The weekly review surfaces drift early — before you've lost a month.

## Tools to use

- **Notion**: duplicate a daily-log template; create a Database with these properties: Date, Hours, Aptitude Qs, DSA Qs, Mood, Mistakes.
- **Google Sheets**: 1 row per day; 1 row per week summary.
- **Plain paper notebook**: works just fine. Sometimes better, because no notifications.

Pick **one** tool. Use it for 30 days before considering a switch.
