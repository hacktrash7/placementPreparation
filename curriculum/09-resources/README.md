# 09 — Resources

A curated list. Use this *only* to pick your **one primary + one secondary** resource per topic. Don't doom-scroll for 5 hours collecting more lists.

## Where to find ESSAYS for practice (Tech M / Wipro / Capgemini)

Common essay prompts:

- AI taking over jobs.
- Online vs offline learning.
- Importance of communication skills.
- Should engineering be 4 years or 3?
- Work-from-home: pros and cons.
- Social media's impact on youth.
- Importance of soft skills in IT industry.
- Indian education system: reforms needed?
- Climate change and engineering.
- Why ethics matter in tech.

Practice 1 essay every Saturday from Week 4. Word target 250-300, time target 25 min. Have someone (peer or ChatGPT) review for structure + grammar.

## A 5-minute weekly resources audit

Every Sunday ask:
- Am I using more than **2** primary resources per topic? If yes, drop one.
- Is the platform helping me **solve problems** or just consume content?
- Have I closed any tab / channel that I haven't used in 14 days? Do it now.

Less is more. Two well-used resources beat 10 saved-and-forgotten ones.
