# 01 — Aptitude

Aptitude is the **single highest-ROI** module for service-MNC placements. Spend 45-60 minutes on it **every single day** from Week 1 to placement day.

## Why aptitude decides your offer

- Roughly **40-50%** of weightage in TCS NQT, Infy, Tech Mahindra, Wipro, Cognizant tests is non-coding aptitude.
- The pool of questions across companies is *largely the same*. Once you've drilled ~1,500 questions, you've seen the patterns.
- It is the most **trainable** section — pure pattern recognition, no creativity required.

## Three sub-modules

| File                                             | What it covers                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [`quantitative-aptitude.md`](quantitative-aptitude.md) | Numbers, percentages, ratios, time-work, P&C, probability, DI |
| [`logical-reasoning.md`](logical-reasoning.md)         | Series, blood relations, coding-decoding, syllogisms, puzzles |
| [`verbal-ability.md`](verbal-ability.md)               | Grammar, vocab, reading comp, sentence correction, Versant     |

## How to drill

1. **Theory first, but only once per topic.** Watch 1 short video (15-20 min) or read 1 chapter. Don't binge.
2. **Solve 15-20 questions on that topic immediately.** Time yourself: ~60-90 sec per question.
3. **Review every wrong answer.** Write the *trick* into your error log.
4. **Next day:** do 5 new questions on the previous day's topic — that's your spaced recall.
5. **Every Sunday:** 30-min sectional mock covering everything you've learned so far.

## Recommended (free) resources — pick ONE primary

- **IndiaBix** — huge bank, slightly old style, free, perfect for volume. *(recommended primary.)*
- **PrepInsta** — has TCS-NQT / Infy-pattern previous year papers. Free tier is enough.
- **GeeksforGeeks Aptitude** — clean explanations.
- **Faceprep** — good topic videos on YouTube.
- **YouTube channels:** Feel Free To Learn, CareerRide, Aptitude Mafia.
- **One book (optional but loved):** *Quantitative Aptitude* by R.S. Aggarwal.

> Don't use more than **one primary + one backup**. Resource-hopping kills aptitude prep.

## 90-day aptitude target

| Period           | Goal                                                              |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Days 1-30        | Cover all 22 quant topics + 12 reasoning topics, ≥600 Qs solved.  |
| Days 31-60       | Revise + verbal/English added, ≥1,200 Qs total.                   |
| Days 61-90       | Speed drills + full mocks, ≥1,800 Qs total, 80%+ accuracy.        |
| After Day 90     | 30-50 Qs/day maintenance + 1 full mock weekly.                    |

You don't need to memorise. You need to **see patterns enough times** that solutions become reflexes. Move on to the sub-files.
