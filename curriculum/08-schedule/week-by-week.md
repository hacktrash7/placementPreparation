# 24-Week Day-by-Day Schedule

Each week balances **aptitude + coding/DSA + 1 CS subject + 1 bonus area**. Time per day: ~4 hours weekdays, ~6-7 weekends.

## How to read this table

Each cell tells you the **theme** for that week. The actual daily target is:
- 45 min Aptitude (rotating between Quant / Reasoning / Verbal)
- 75 min Coding / DSA
- 45 min CS Subject
- 45 min Bonus / Project
- 15 min revision

## What to do if you fall behind

The first time you fall behind by 1-2 days:

- **Skip the bonus track** for the catch-up days (keep aptitude + DSA + CS).
- Spend the next Saturday morning doing the missed material.
- Don't extend past Week 24 — instead, *trim Block 11 / 12* in DSA.

If you fall behind by more than a week:

- Do an honest sit-down with this curriculum.
- Identify the bottleneck (aptitude? coding? motivation?).
- Cut to 3-priority mode: **aptitude + DSA + 1 CS subject** until you're back on track. Drop bonus skills temporarily.

Discipline > talent. You can recover. Just don't quit.
