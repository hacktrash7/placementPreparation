# 07 — Interview Preparation

Once you clear the online test, **3 short interviews** stand between you and an offer:

1. **Technical Interview** — DSA, CS theory, project deep-dive.
2. **Managerial / HR-Technical Interview** — situational, fit, scenario, light tech.
3. **HR Interview** — culture, communication, salary, joining.

This folder prepares you for each.

## The fundamental mindset

> Interviews are **structured conversations**, not interrogations. The interviewer wants to see how you *think*, not whether you spit out the perfect answer.

Three principles to absorb deeply:

1. **Think out loud.** Silence is your enemy. Narrate your thought process even if it's wrong — the interviewer can guide you.
2. **Ask clarifying questions.** "Should the input handle negatives?" "Can I assume the array fits in memory?" Always. It's a sign of seniority.
3. **It's OK not to know.** Saying "I'm not sure, but my best guess is X based on Y" is far better than freezing or bluffing.

## What to carry to the actual interview

- 2 printed copies of your **resume**.
- A **government photo ID** (Aadhaar / driving licence / passport).
- A pen + a small notebook.
- A **water bottle**.
- All certificates / mark sheets (services MNCs collect copies during the offer round).

For virtual interviews:
- Charged laptop + charger + Wi-Fi backup (phone hotspot).
- Quiet room, neutral background, decent lighting.
- Headphones with mic (NOT the laptop mic).
- Webcam at eye level.
- Resume PDF open in another tab + GitHub.
- Code editor open in case live coding is asked.
